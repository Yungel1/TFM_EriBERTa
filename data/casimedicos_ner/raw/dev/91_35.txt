QUESTION TYPE: GENERAL SURGERY
CLINICAL CASE: 
A 26-year-old woman presenting with fecal incontinence after a prolonged instrumental delivery. An endoanal ultrasound was performed showing a section of the external anal sphincter of 30ยบ amplitude. The electrophysiological study shows normal innervation. What is the most indicated treatment?
1- Medical treatment with hygienic and dietary norms.
2- Sphincter biofeedback.
3- Surgical sphincteroplasty.
4- Surgical repair of the pelvic floor.
5- Artificial anal sphincter.
CORRECT ANSWER: 3
The answer is surgical sphincteroplasty 3. 1 is not effective, 2 is not effective for an organic lesion with normal electromyography, 4 is not necessary since the lesion is only of the external sphincter, 5 is not indicated in a lesion that can be repaired with surgery with good results.
