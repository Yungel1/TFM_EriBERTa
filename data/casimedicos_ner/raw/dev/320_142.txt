CORRECT ANSWER: 3
It is a chondrocalcinosis. There is no treatment for this disease, only treatment for flare-ups (colchicine, NSAIDs or corticosteroids), although sometimes if the flare-ups are habitual the colchicine is maintained. Allopurinol has not shown any efficacy in this microcrystal arthropathy, but it has shown efficacy in gout.
