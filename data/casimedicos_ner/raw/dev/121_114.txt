QUESTION TYPE: MICROBIOLOGY
CLINICAL CASE: 
47-year-old woman. Native of Cochabamba (Bolivia), she has been living in Spain for 8 years. In mid 2009 she began to experience palpitations, dyspnea and progressive edema in the lower extremities. She was diagnosed with heart failure secondary to cardiomyopathy due to Chagas disease. What is the etiological agent of this disease endemic in several Latin American countries?
1- Toxoplasma gondii.
2- Trypanosoma brucei.
3- Lehismania donovani.
4- Giardia lamblia.
5- Trypanosoma cruzi.
CORRECT ANSWER: 5
The correct answer is the fifth one. The cause of Chagas disease is Trypanosoma cruzi.
