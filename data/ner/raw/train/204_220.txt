QUESTION TYPE: GENETICS AND IMMUNOLOGY
CLINICAL CASE: 
A 43-year-old man consults for diarrheal syndrome, and refers among his history 3 pneumonias in adulthood. Which of the following immunological studies should we request:
1- Serum immunoglobulin count and antibody production capacity test.
2- Phagocytosis test and oxidative metabolism of neutrophils.
3- Apoptosis test (programmed cell death) in the patient's circulating lymphocytes.
4- Study of the repertoire and clonality of T lymphocytes (alpha/beta).
5- In this patient it would not be appropriate to request any immunological study.
CORRECT ANSWER: 1
More than 2 lobar pneumonias make it necessary to rule out an immunologic deficit.
